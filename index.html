〈Welcome to Teacup!〉

What is teacup?

Teacup is part of an Engame Academy Sophomore project, with initial creation launching in the year 2023. The content is based on the chinese and japanese tea ceremonies, albeit the site's main aim is to accomodate the project's documentation, which is in connection to web development.

The site is an important part of the project, hence it still serves as a place to get informed and learn about tea culture, as well as read reviews and see a gallery of different teas. The site will be expanding and updated regularly as time passes.

For more information about the project, see to the About page.
